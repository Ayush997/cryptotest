What is Corda?

Corda is an open source distributed ledger and blockchain platform designed for digital assets and financial sectors.

It enables users to create, issue and trade tokens, address private transactions, develop digital assets, streamline attestation processes throughout the identity provision lifecycle, as well as protect data storage with transparent reporting for regulatory management.

Corda architecture is designed to model and automate real-world transactions in a legally enforceable manner,
and do so across an open network on which multiple applications can execute and seamlessly interoperate.

Why Corda?

Point-to-Point Architecture


Communication on Corda Blockchain Platform is point-to-point , i.e., only participants of transactions can access it.


Multilateral Ledger



Unlike other blockchain platforms, Corda Blockchain only allows participants to have copies of transactions they are observers of.

Pluggable Consensus



With multiple uniqueness services, Corda can utilize several algorithms and transactions can scale because notary events can partition across the network.
