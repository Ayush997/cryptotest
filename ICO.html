What is ICO?

ICO commonly known as Initial Coin Offerings are a popular fundraising method used primarily by startups
wishing to offer products and services, usually related to the cryptocurrency and blockchain space.

In this fundraising mechanism, new projects sell their underlying crypto tokens in exchange for bitcoin and ether.
ICOs are somewhat similar to stocks, but having utility for a software service or product offered.
